University Transport Rules & Regulations

  • All passengers must carry a valid university ID card.
  • Seats are allocated on a first-come, first-served basis.
  • Smoking and eating are strictly prohibited inside the bus.
  • Students must arrive at the bus stop at least 5 minutes before departure.
  • Respect the driver and follow their instructions at all times.
  • Misbehavior or vandalism will result in disciplinary action.
  • Keep noise levels low to avoid disturbing other passengers.
  • Report any lost items to the university transport office.
